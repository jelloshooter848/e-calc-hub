Hand Bending

Master the fundamentals of conduit hand bending techniques

Course Overview

Comprehensive 24-hour training program covering all essential hand bending techniques. Learn proper bender component terminology, mathematical calculations, and progressive skill development across four intensive Saturday sessions.

Course Duration

24 hours over 4 consecutive Saturdays

Session Length

6 hours per Saturday session

Training Format

Progressive hands-on skill building

Status

Available Now

What You'll Learn

1 First Saturday

  • Bender components and terminology
  • Essential Math and Trigonometry
  • 90-degree bends
  • Back-to-back 90-degree bends
  • Compound 90-degree bends

2 Second Saturday

  • Offset bend techniques
  • Rolling offset applications

3 Third Saturday

  • 3-point saddle construction
  • 4-point saddle techniques
  • Kick 90-degree bends

4 Fourth Saturday

  • Parallel offset techniques
  • Parallel kick 90-degree bends
