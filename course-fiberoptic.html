Fiber Optic Training (FOA)

CFOT certification preparation with comprehensive hands-on training

Course Overview

Comprehensive fiber optic training designed to prepare participants for the CFOT (Certified Fiber Optic Technician) exam offered by the FOA (Fiber Optic Association). This program combines in-class learning with extensive hands-on classroom sessions to provide complete certification preparation.

Certification Goal

CFOT (Certified Fiber Optic Technician)

Education Credits

Up to 32 CEUs available

Course Hours

33 continuing education hours

Status

Available Now

Important: $65 refundable class deposit required. The FOA certification can be taken for credit every three years.

What You'll Learn

Theory and Fundamentals

  • Fiber optic principles and physics
  • Types of fiber optic cables and components
  • Network design and architecture
  • Safety procedures and protocols

Practical Applications

  • Cable installation techniques
  • Splicing and termination methods
  • Testing and troubleshooting procedures
  • Three required hands-on practice sessions

Certification Requirements

Course Completion

  • Complete all in-class learning sessions
  • Attend three mandatory hands-on sessions
  • Pass the CFOT certification test

Certification Benefits

  • Industry-recognized FOA certification
  • Up to 32 continuing education units
  • Renewable every three years
