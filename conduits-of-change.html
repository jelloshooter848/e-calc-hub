About Conduits of Change

Local Union Focus

Conduits of Change is IBEW Local 332's quarterly newsletter, delivering essential updates about our local union, member achievements, and regional electrical industry developments.

Published four times a year, each issue highlights the vibrant community of electrical professionals in our region and celebrates the successes of our members.

What You'll Find

  • Local union news and updates
  • Member spotlights and achievements
  • Regional industry developments
  • Training and apprenticeship updates
