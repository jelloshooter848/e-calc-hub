Bluebeam - Intro to Design

Advanced electrical design theory with digital 2D detailing using Bluebeam Revu

Course Overview

Advanced 11-week electrical design course (32 hours) covering electrical design theory with a focus on digital design and 2D detailing using Bluebeam Revu. This intermediate-level program requires previous Bluebeam experience and Windows competency, meeting Tuesday and Thursday evenings.

Course Duration

11 weeks (32 total hours)

Schedule

Tuesday & Thursday evenings

Course Level

Intermediate (Prerequisites required)

Status

Available Now

Prerequisites Required: Previous Bluebeam Revu experience and Windows computer competency are essential for success in this intermediate course.

What You'll Learn

Electrical Design Theory

  • Advanced electrical system design principles
  • Load calculations and circuit analysis
  • Electrical code applications in design
  • Power distribution system planning

Digital Design & 2D Detailing

  • Advanced Bluebeam Revu techniques
  • 2D electrical drawing and detailing
  • Digital markup and annotation tools
  • Professional drawing standards and practices

11-Week Course Structure

Weeks 1-6: Foundation

  • Electrical design fundamentals review
  • Advanced Bluebeam Revu tools and features
  • Digital drawing standards and protocols

Weeks 7-11: Application

  • Complex electrical system design projects
  • Professional 2D detailing techniques
  • Portfolio development and final project

Evening Schedule: Classes meet Tuesday and Thursday evenings, providing flexibility for working professionals to advance their electrical design skills.
