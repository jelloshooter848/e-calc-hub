Microsoft Office 365 Intensive

40-hour comprehensive training in Microsoft productivity suite

Course Overview

This 10-class course will provide an in-depth review of the functionality and best practices for Microsoft Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, Teams, and Planner. While there are no required pre-requisites for this class, a basic knowledge of computers will provide maximum benefit for this course.

All course content will be completed in the computer lab with hands-on instruction and practice.

Course Topics

Core Applications

  • Microsoft Word
    Document Creation & Modification, Text Formatting, Multi-Media Integrations
  • Microsoft Excel
    Document Creation & Modification, Cell Formulas & Formatting, Form Tools
  • Microsoft Outlook
    Account/Profile setup, Mail, Calendar, Contacts Management, Signatures

Collaboration Tools

  • Microsoft PowerPoint
    Presentation basics, Transitions & Animations, Multi-Media Integrations
  • Microsoft Planner
    Board & Task setup, Team Assignments & Priorities, Scheduling
  • Microsoft Teams
    Group & Individual messaging, Screen & Content sharing
