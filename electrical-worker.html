About The Electrical Worker

Historic Publication

The Electrical Worker has been the official publication of the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers since 1893, making it one of the longest-running labor publications in North America.

For over 130 years, it has served as the primary communication channel between IBEW International and its members across the United States and Canada.

Monthly Coverage

  • National IBEW policy and developments
  • Industry trends and legislative updates
  • Local union highlights from across North America
  • Safety updates and training opportunities
